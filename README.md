# 📊 Retail Sales Performance Analysis

Project 01 of my Data Analytics Portfolio, focused on Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and business-oriented insights.

## 📌 Project Description

This project performs an end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail sales dataset using Python.

The objective is to identify sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and business opportunities through data visualization and statistical analysis.

The analysis follows a complete data analytics workflow, from data cleaning and feature engineering to business-oriented insights and recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze monthly sales performance.
- Identify the most profitable product categories.
- Explore the relationship between sales and profit.
- Detect customer purchasing patterns.
- Evaluate payment methods and device preferences.
- Generate actionable business recommendations.

---

## 📈 Exploratory Analysis 

The notebook includes the following analyses:

- Monthly Sales Trend

- Profit Margin Analysis
- Sales vs Profit Relationship
- Correlation Matrix
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Sales by Device Type
- Sales by Payment Method
- Orders by Priority

Each section includes:

- Data preparation
- Visualization
- Business insight

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Sales followed an overall upward trend throughout the year.
- Fashion generated both the highest profit and the highest profit margin.
- Sales and Profit presented a very strong positive correlation (0.92).
- Credit cards represented the dominant payment method.
- Most customers were registered members, highlighting strong customer retention.
- Web purchases exceeded mobile purchases.
- Customer distribution was balanced between male and female shoppers.
- Medium-priority orders accounted for most transactions.
